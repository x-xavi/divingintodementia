What's Next?

If we do go through with this project, we are hoping to actually volunteer and work with dementia patients, such as at Momentia. We want to make a meaningful difference for people with dementia, and support them even through their changing state.

2. Spicing Up the Website

When it comes to online design, there's a variety of different possibilities, and we want to branch out and try different things. We want to add more about our experience, add more interactive parts, and overall make this website more informative and enjoyable.
